## What Is Webhosting1st?

Webhosting1st is a hosting company founded in 2018 (with roots in the industry going back to 2011). They're based in Poland and run a Tier III data center with DDoS protection, daily backups, and RAID storage. The target audience is pretty clear: people and small businesses who want solid, no-frills hosting without spending a lot of money.

The control panel they use is **DirectAdmin** — not cPanel, which some people miss, but DirectAdmin is genuinely capable and plenty fast. You get the full Softaculous one-click installer, meaning WordPress, Joomla, Drupal, Magento, PrestaShop — all just a click away.

All plans include:
- **Pure NVMe SSD storage** (not spinning disk, not regular SSD)
- **Unlimited domains** and subdomains
- **Unlimited email accounts**
- **Unlimited MySQL databases**
- **Free Let's Encrypt SSL**
- **Daily backups**
- **DDoS protection**
- **99.9% uptime guarantee**
- **24/7 support**

That's not a budget-tier feature list. That's actually pretty generous.

## What Actual Users Say

Third-party review sites paint a pretty consistent picture. Over at HostAdvice, one user running a medium-traffic WordPress blog noted that they were happy with server speeds and found the price unbeatable compared to other options. On WebsitePlanet, another user mentioned that the DirectAdmin panel made managing multiple domains surprisingly straightforward, and the Softaculous one-click installer was easier to use than what they'd experienced with previous hosts.

There's one consistent wrinkle that pops up: **live chat response times** can be slow, or occasionally unresponsive. The ticket-based support tends to be more reliable. So if you're someone who needs instant hand-holding, keep that in mind. But if you're comfortable with self-serve and can use their 35-article knowledge base, you'll likely be fine.

There was one negative review worth noting — a VPS user had their account suspended after being flagged for spam email activity. The company's response clarified it was a mass-email violation. It's a reminder that like all reputable hosts, Webhosting1st enforces anti-spam policies.

Overall rating on WHTop: **8.4/10**, with 6 out of 7 reviewers recommending the service.
